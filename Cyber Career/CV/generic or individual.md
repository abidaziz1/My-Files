## CUSTOMIZATION TIPS FOR EACH APPLICATION:

*Research Before Writing:*
1. Visit company website and read "About Us" section
2. Check their blog for recent security posts
3. Look at their tech stack (from job posting or LinkedIn)
4. Find hiring manager on LinkedIn
5. Note company values/culture

*Mirror Their Language:*
- If they say "red teaming" → use "red teaming" (not "penetration testing")
- If they mention specific tools → highlight your experience with those tools
- Match their tone (formal vs. casual)

*Customize These Sections Every Time:*
- [ ] Company name (appears 4-6 times)
- [ ] Specific job requirements you're addressing
- [ ] Why you're interested in THIS company specifically
- [ ] Relevant skills/tools from job posting
- [ ] Your most relevant achievement for THIS role

*Keep These Consistent:*
- ✓ Your core qualifications (CEH Master, experience metrics)
- ✓ Remote work capabilities
- ✓ Professional tone
- ✓ Clear structure and formatting

*Length:* Aim for 3-4 short paragraphs max. Hiring managers spend 30 seconds scanning cover letters.

*File Format:* Save as PDF named: "YourName_CoverLetter_CompanyName.pdf"
